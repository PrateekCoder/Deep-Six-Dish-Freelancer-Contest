ABOUT

PROFESSIONAL SATELLITE DISH REMOVAL

Dish Disposal removes unwanted satellite dishes and antennas across the country with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. We are committed to improving the appearance and safety of your home or business by removing all shapes, sizes, and ages of satellite dishes and antennas.

SATELLITE AND ANTENNA REMOVAL SERVICES AND PRICING

Dish Disposal removes and recycles all kinds of unsightly satellite dish receivers and antenna's from residential and commercial properties. Our removal services include standard satellite dishes, large satellite dishes, small TV antenna's and very tall antenna towers. See each page for additional details.

RESPONSIBILITY OF SATELLITE DISH REMOVAL

At Dish Disposal we take pride in our work and your satisfaction is 100% guaranteed. Our local Authorized Partners are licensed, bonded, and insured as required in each locale and you can rest assured that the materials removed from your property will be recycled/disposed of as required by law.
